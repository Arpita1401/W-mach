Project brief

Target Problem

Starting from teens to old agers, a whole lot of people are suffering from body pain now-a-days. Due to digitalization, working officials were already suffering from pain from sitting infront of screens all day. In this addition to this,pandemic has caused the youth and kids to sit across computers too. AS a result of which, many-a-people are suffering from pain, which can lead to diseases like spondilytes and more.

What needs to be done?

First of all, people need to acknowledge the pain and not just ignore it. According to medical professionals, hot water bags can be really helpful.But often, we don't have the time to just lay down for the water bags, or we have some or other woek to do. Due to this ,we have come up with W-mach.

What is W-mach?

W-mach is a waearable machinery- a jacket, that will give us relief from the bodypain. It can be worn anytime and anywhere.Additionally, the temperature can be managed by connecting the jacket with your phone via bluetooth.

What more?

We also do have the facility for the people to send their issue through our website, which will reach the assigned medical professionals and they can get advices sent to their email accounts.

Statistics of people suffering from body pain across different age groups

0 The highest number of people going through body pain every minute in India is working class persons.
0 Due to less access to doctors in this pandemic in India, there has been a growth in number of senior citizens suffering due to the pain
0 There has been a recent rise in count of teenagers & kids suffering from back pain in India

Why is our project necessary?

One thing can lead to other

It has been found out in a recent study that people who suffer from back pain produce less output than the perfectly healthy persons, due to obvious reasons;which in turn has led to the feeling of worthlessness, sadness and a great amount of nervousness in them. These all have amounted to depression and loneliness.

Types of pain and the growth rate

There are different types of back pain found in humans due to various reasons in different age groups.These are cervical back pain, low back pain, neck pain,etc. Some suffer due to sitting infront of screens all day, while some suffer due to old age. Anything may the reason be, this is a disease that should not be neglected, or else it can lead to even a bigger disease.

Injuries during sports

Athletes-sports persons often get their body sprained during the sports. While sprays can give temporary healing, hot water bags are necessary to give a long term relief, which is when our tool will come in handy, as it can be worn anytime and anywhere- they can wear it while doing their other chores.
